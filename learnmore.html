LEARN MORE



While technology improves access to mental health services, it is ironically also contributing to more mental illness. Since people spend so much time on devices, they’re less likely to have meaningful social interactions. Subsequently, addictive patterns and dopamine boost lead to a never-ending vicious cycle and strong dependence on technology.
